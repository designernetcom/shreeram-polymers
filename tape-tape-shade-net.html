Tape*Tape Shade Net

Shade nets or garden nets are widely used in gardens, greenhouses, parking, gardens, fencing, window, gallery, balcony, roofs, outdoor curtains, flooring, construction site cover, parking cover, etc., for dust protection, sun protection, and safety.

Shade nets are ventilated climate controllers that work great to keep the heat out and protect your plants from the sunlight and overheating in greenhouses. It is the most effective way to protect against UV harm and nurture healthy, productive plants. Shreeram Polymers nets are produced using a specific combination of HDPE, Master Batch, UV stabilities, and functional additives to make them durable and long-lasting, providing a guaranteed 3-5 years of UV life for the product.

Applications

These nets are fully capable of filtering & regulating the sunlight minimizing the direct contact with the flora and fauna and keeping their environ in a placid state.

The shade nets accomplish the task of providing required microclimate condition to the plants.

We craft shade nets according to industry standards and design it in a way that facilitates the ease of installation.

  • Nursery Shade Net
  • Green House
  • Vehicle /Car Parking Net
  • Tent House
  • Construction Net
  • Mandap Decoration
  • Polyhouse etc

Technical Information

Shreeram Polymers Shade Nets are manufactured from high density polyethylene (HDPE) and Treated with anti Sulphur, UV Stabilizer, and functional additives using warp knitting technology.

These nets come with different shade percentages. The shading percentage will decide how much percent of light intensity will be cut down by that particular shade net.
