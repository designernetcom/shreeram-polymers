Technical Information

Shreeram Polymers Mono*Mono Shade Nets are manufactured from high density polyethylene (HDPE) and these materials offer durability, UV resistance, and strength. The knitting pattern of the shade net determines its strength and durability.

Monofilament shade nets usually have a knitted construction for added strength and stability. It's important for monofilament shade nets to be UV stabilized to ensure longevity and protection against UV degradation, especially in outdoor applications where they are exposed to sunlight for extended periods.
